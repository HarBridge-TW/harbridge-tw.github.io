Industry Applications

Our machinery serves a wide range of industries, including machine shops and OEMs, civil aviation, automotive, medical, injection and die casting molds, and more.

  • • Machine Shops & OEMs
    We provide high-precision solutions for machine shops and OEMs to streamline production and improve accuracy.
  • • Civil Aviation
    Our machinery meets the strict requirements of the aviation industry, ensuring reliable performance and precision.
  • • Automotive
    We support automotive manufacturing with efficient tooling and machining solutions for engine parts, chassis, and more.
  • • Medical
    Our technology enables the creation of precise medical devices and components, adhering to industry regulations.
  • • Injection & Die Casting Molds
    We offer robust equipment for mold makers, ensuring durability and high-quality surface finishes.
  • • Others
    Our flexible systems can be adapted to various industries beyond the above, including energy and electronics.
